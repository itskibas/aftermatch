Safety Standards & Child Protection Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2024

Our Commitment to User Safety

At AfterMatch, we are committed to creating a safe, respectful, and secure environment for all our users. We have zero tolerance for any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) or inappropriate behavior targeting minors.

Age Restrictions & Verification

  • Minimum Age Requirement: Users must be 18 years or older to use AfterMatch
  • Age Verification: We implement age verification measures to prevent underage users from accessing our platform
  • Parental Controls: We encourage parents to monitor their children's online activities and use appropriate parental control tools

Content Moderation & Safety Measures

Prohibited Content

  • Any content involving minors in sexual contexts
  • Explicit sexual content or nudity
  • Harassment, bullying, or threatening behavior
  • Impersonation or fake profiles
  • Commercial solicitation or spam
  • Illegal activities or content

Safety Features

  • Content Filtering: Automated systems to detect and remove inappropriate content
  • User Reporting: Easy-to-use reporting system for violations
  • Blocking: Users can block and report other users
  • Photo Verification: Optional photo verification to ensure authentic profiles
  • Chat Monitoring: AI-powered chat monitoring for inappropriate content

Reporting & Enforcement

How to Report Violations

  • Use the in-app reporting feature for any suspicious activity
  • Report inappropriate messages, photos, or behavior
  • Contact our support team for urgent safety concerns

24-Hour Moderation Commitment

We commit to reviewing and taking action on all reported content within 24 hours. Our dedicated moderation team works around the clock to ensure the safety of our community.

Enforcement Actions

  • Immediate Response: All reports reviewed within 24 hours
  • Content Removal: Objectionable content removed immediately upon verification
  • User Suspension: Immediate account suspension for serious violations
  • Permanent Ban: Permanent account termination for severe or repeated violations
  • User Ejection: Users who post objectionable content are permanently removed from the platform
  • Law Enforcement: Cooperation with law enforcement when required
  • Policy Updates: Regular review and updates of safety policies

Legal Compliance

  • Compliance with all applicable child protection laws
  • Adherence to international standards for online safety
  • Regular legal reviews of our safety policies
  • Cooperation with law enforcement agencies
  • Compliance with GDPR and other privacy regulations

User Education & Prevention

  • Safety tips and guidelines for users
  • Educational content about online safety
  • Regular reminders about safe online behavior
  • Resources for users who need help or support

⚠️ Important Safety Reminder

If you encounter any content that appears to involve minors in inappropriate contexts, please report it immediately. Your safety and the safety of all users is our top priority.

Contact Information

For Safety Concerns: Use the in-app reporting feature or contact our support team

For Law Enforcement: We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations

For Parents/Guardians: Contact us if you have concerns about your child's use of our platform
